1. What is Mona Digital Platform?

Mona is a digital platform that serves as a one-stop solution for local businesses and customers in Kigali, Rwanda. Our objective is to develop a groundbreaking platform that facilitates meaningful connections between businesses and customers. This platform will serve as a bridge, enabling businesses to showcase their offerings and engage with a diverse customer base. By providing a centralized hub for information and interaction, we aim to enhance customer engagement and support business growth.
